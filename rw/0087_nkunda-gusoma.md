# Nkunda gusoma!

##
Nkunda gusoma.

##
Ninde nasomera?

##
Mushiki wange araryamye.

##
Ninde nasomera?

##
Mama wange na nyogokuru wange barahuze.

##
Ninde nasomera?

##
Dada wange na sogokuru wange barahuze.

##
Ninde nasomera? Ndisomera!
