# ̃ ĩnũ wé lẽ Drĩꞌ̣ bị́ Iz

##
Tụ́ li ̣̃ ꞌbã driꞌbi
̣̃ ̣́ ã liŋá
̣́


##
Ánã ꞌbã driꞌbi
̣̃ ̣́ ã zo


##
Ká ti ̣̃ ꞌbã driꞌbi
̣̃ ̣́ zo ã ndá ni ̃


##
Zá mã cã driꞌbi
̣̃ ̣́ i ̃ꞌdidri ̣̂
cã cã


##
Bã bá tiꞌbi
̣́ ̣̂ trũ


##
Zã né lẽ nze driꞌbi
̣̃ ̣́ i ̃ꞌdidri ̣̂
nzẽ -nzẽ .


##
Tá bõ li ̣ driꞌbi
̣̃ ̣́ i ̃ꞌdidri ̣̂ rá .


##
Té mbã ufá driꞌbi
̣̃ ̣́ i ̃ꞌdidri ̣̂
rá .
